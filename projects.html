How I Work

These projects come from a consistent process: understand the problem, structure the system, make the interface clear, validate the technical core, and keep the output usable.

01 • Problem Framing

I start from the actual system need

Whether it is a route-planning product, an HPC environment, or an IoT intelligence workflow, I begin by understanding what the system must achieve and what the user or operator must be able to do with it.

02 • Architecture

I define the moving parts clearly

I think in states, components, services, and data flows. That is especially important in projects like Derech and SLURM-based systems where clarity of interaction and coordination matters.

03 • Delivery

I build for demonstration and reliability

I care about polish, but not shallow polish. The goal is always to produce work that can be shown, explained, tested, and improved with confidence.
